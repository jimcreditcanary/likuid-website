About Likuid

We're building better outcomes across the credit journey.

Likuid brings AI credit decisioning and payments infrastructure together on one platform — so lenders can manage risk, make smarter decisions, and get paid, all in one place.

Our mission

Credit and payments have always been treated as separate problems, solved by separate systems. That disconnect is where risk hides and opportunity is lost. Likuid was formed to close the gap — uniting decisioning and payments on a single data layer so every payment outcome sharpens the next credit decision, and every decision improves how lenders get paid.

By connecting every stage of the credit journey — from onboarding and verification to decisioning, payments and collections — we give lenders complete visibility and control, with real-time data, automation and intelligent insight in one platform.

What we stand for

Better decisions

Real-time, explainable decisioning that learns continuously — approving more of the right customers, at the right terms.

Fairer outcomes

Proactive engagement and automated support that treats customers fairly and intervenes before problems escalate.

Trust by design

Bank-grade security and compliance built in from day one — ISO 27001, Cyber Essentials and GDPR by default.

A team with a shared passion for financial services
